About Us

Repower Energy Development Corporation (REDC) is a 100% owned subsidiary of Pure Energy Holdings Corporation. REDC is renewable energy developer of run of river hydropower projects through a "clustered approach method" where several projects are in a target area providing for shared economies of scale through common infrastructure, reducing the cost of development and improving the bottom line.

Advantages of run of river hydropower Projects

Philippines’ run-of-the-river hydropower: minimal social impact, no barangay submersion, and high social acceptance

Enhances the Forest Cover of the Watershed

DOE requires watershed plans post-contract. Hydropower developers prioritize forest conservation for water quality and quantity

Saving in CO2 Emissions on a Global Scale

Savings by the Philippine Government from Importation of Oil

A 1MW mini-hydro plant in the Philippines can save US$500,000 per year in oil imports and is incentivized by the Energy Act of 2008 (RA 9513)
